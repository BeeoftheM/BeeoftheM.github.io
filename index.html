Welcome to the Augmented Reality website for Kelham Island! This will allow you to scan markers like QR codes and
see a textbox that gives a brief summary of an exhibit. These summaries could be a better alternative to reading signs
that have bigger amounts of text and may not keep your attention for long.

Note: This website is not an official Kelham Island website and has not been made by Kelham Island employees.
